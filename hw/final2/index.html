Welcome to Sweet!

Sweet is an online reference point for dessert lovers who are both native or traveling to Los Angeles. This website highlights a few dessert places in LA that are delicious and must trys! You will find the location, price, what each place brings to the table (what type of person each place is best for), and more! Enjoy!

Bigg Chill

Bigg Chill is a frozen yogurt place open everyday from 9am-11pm located in a very central part of West LA making it very easy for people in all areas to get there. This frozen yogurt place is extremely popular, especially from 8pm-11pm, due to its variety of toppings, including edible cookie dough, and flavors that change every Monday Wednesday and Friday. There are dairy free, low calorie, and normal flavors to try and appeal to everyone. Although it may seem a bit pricy, being $5 for a small, $5.50 for a medium, a large is $6.25, a $7.25 pint, and $9.25 for a quart, the portions are very big! One thing to note is Bigg Chill is cash only but not to worry if you dont have cash on you as there is an ATM inside!

Salt and Straw

Salt and straw is a very unique ice cream place with many locations but this is focusing on the West Hollywood location open everyday from 11am-11pm. This icecream place is located in a very fun part of Los Angeles so eating ice cream and people watching is a must! The flavors are extremely original and you are not likely to find any flavors like these at another place. A single scoop is $4.95,a double is $6.95, a kids' scoop is $3.95, and pint is $10.50. The atmosphere is very fun and everybody getting their ice cream is so happy, including the workers! Salt and straw is very busy from 9pm-10:30pm and has a line going out the door! There are vegan flavors and regular flavors to try to appeal to everyone. This icecream place is for a more adventurous person who wants to try some crazy flavors, although they still serve a great vanilla!

Diddy Riese

Diddy Riese is located in the heart of Westwood. It is cheap, delicious, and a los angeles staple! It is great for college students due to its inexpensive price, its close proximity to UCLA, and it is open much later than most places as its hours are from 11am-12pm. The cookies are very high quality and the icecream is Dreyers! For those who do not love cookies, icecream, or are allergic to dairy there is the option to get shaved ice instead! Being a very popular spot in LA, be ready to wait in a long line but it is all worth it when you get your dessert!

Kith

For the fashion lovers and ice cream lovers, Kith is your place! It is located on Sunset Blvd inside a fred segal and is surrounded by great restaurants. Kith is both an ice cream store and a clothing store with many high end brands. The icream is on the more expensive side but it is a verrryy big portion. It is open from 10am-9pm and the price start at $6.50 with 2 complimentary mix ins and each additional mix in is an extra $.75. There is rarely a long line as people scatter in and out throughout the day! There are four base flavors to choose from, vanilla, chocolate, vanilla coconut, and chocolate coconut making more dairy free flavors! This place is not your typical icecream store but the experience and taste is well worth the price!
