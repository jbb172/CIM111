Homemade Oreos!

From a young age my family has been making these cookies for all our family events because they truly are the best. I would request them for birthdays, we would have them at Thanksgiving dinner, or just make them for fun because they are delicious. It takes about 30 minutes in total to make these cookies. It is about 10 minutes of prep time, 8 minutes for the cookies to cook and a few minutes for the cookies to cool down before frosting. They are extremely easy to make and satisfy people of all ages!

Ingredients

click here to change the background and show steps

  • Chocolate and Vanilla cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 2 cans of vanilla frosting

Steps!

click here to change background to pink

  • Step 1 preheat oven to 350 degrees
  • Step 2 melt 1 stick of butter in a bowl
  • Step 3 in a large bowl mix the chocolate cake, melted butter, one egg, and 1/4 cup of milk
  • Step 4 roll the dough into balls in your hands and place on a cookie sheet
  • Step 5 place into oven then repeat with the vanilla cake
  • Step 6 cook the cookies for 8-10 minutes depending how soft you like your cookies
  • Step 7 wait for cookies to cool then frost one and put another on top like an oreo!
