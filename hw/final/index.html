Bigg Chill

Bigg Chill is a frozen yogurt place located in a very central part of West LA. It is very easy for people that live in Bel Air, Beverly Hills, Westwood, and Brentwood to go to. This frozen yogurt place is extremely popular due to its variety of toppings (including edible cookie dough) and flavors that change every Monday Wednesday and Friday. There are dairy free, low calorie, and normal flavors to try and appeal to everyone. Although it may seem a bit pricy, the portions are very big! One thing to note is Bigg Chill is cash only but not to worry if you dont have cash on you as there is an ATM inside! add more abt when to go (when it is most busy and not), what type of peopel go and make better!

Salt and Straw

Salt and straw is a very unique ice cream place. There are many locations but the one I am focusing on is the one in West Hollywood. This icecream place is located in a very fun part of Los Angeles so eating ice cream and people watching is a guarantee. The flavors are extremely original and you are not likely to find any other flavors like these at another place. add more

  • Location: 8949 Santa Monica Blvd, West Hollywood, CA
  • Hours: everyday 11am-11pm
  • Price: A single scoop is $4.95, double $6.95 and kids' scoop $3.95, pint is $10.50
